04 Market and competitors

Market reference — who to look at (verified, current for 2026):
  • Apps (nationwide): bTaskee, JupViec, eClean. Hourly cleaning, payment by cash / transfer / MoMo / ZaloPay, free re-clean guarantee.
  • Da Nang, local: 3Clean, Song Anh Hygiene. Hourly cleaning, trained staff, transparent prices.
  • Da Nang, for foreigners: Thu Huyen Clean — English-speaking, booking via Zalo, staff background checks, move-in/out and Airbnb. This is your closest analogue.
  • Ho Chi Minh City, premium: TKT Clean, Ayasan, ZenHome24h. “Japanese standard”, for foreigners and businesses.
  • Corporate (B2B): AGS Vietnam — ISO certification, offices and buildings.
